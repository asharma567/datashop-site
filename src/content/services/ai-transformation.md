Most organizations don't need another AI pilot. They need a clear-eyed view of where AI actually moves the business, a plan that reaches past a single team, and leadership alignment on what changes.

We partner with executives and their senior teams to map the business end to end, identify where AI compounds leverage (and where it's a distraction), and stand up the operating model — tooling, org structure, review practices — that makes the change durable.

## What engagements look like

- **Weeks 1–2**: executive workshops, systems mapping, diagnostic interviews across functions
- **Weeks 3–6**: opportunity prioritization, roadmap, governance design, tooling recommendations
- **Weeks 6+**: hands-on build of the two or three highest-leverage initiatives; leadership coaching; measurement

## Who this is for

CEOs, COOs, and function leaders at organizations with real revenue and complexity — not startups looking for a first prototype. If you've already tried point solutions and felt they didn't add up to transformation, we should talk.
