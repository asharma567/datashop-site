The moment your AI product starts working is when the hard problems begin. Latency, cost, evals, data pipelines, observability, the quiet tax of prompts drifting out from under you — none of it shows up in a demo.

We parachute in when you've proven the idea and now need to make it real. We've scaled AI systems through the full arc — from first-hundred-users to production workloads — and we know where the cliffs are.

## What we bring

- **Evaluation infrastructure** so you can move fast without regressing
- **Cost and latency engineering** — right-sizing models, caching, fallback chains, batching
- **Data pipelines** that don't quietly break when the distribution shifts
- **Observability** you can actually debug from
- **Production practices** — versioning, rollback, canaries — adapted to how AI systems actually fail

## Who this is for

Product teams with a working prototype (internal or external) that's getting traction, and an honest realization that the path from here to "100x the users" isn't going to be smooth without help.
